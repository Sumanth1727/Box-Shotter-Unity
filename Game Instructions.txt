                                                                BOX SHOOTER

There are 3 types of box
blue box  - by hitting this your score increases by 5
white box - by hitting this your time is increased by +3 seconds
yellow box -  by hitting this your time is decreased by 10 seconds

At the start of the game you have 15 secconds.try hiiting white boxes to increase the time and try hitting the blue boxs to increase 
the score.
try avoiding the yellow boxes
you can control the player using MOUSE and WSAD keys
if the time run out it is game over
hit the play again 3D text to start the game again
